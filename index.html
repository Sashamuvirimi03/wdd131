About Me

The world is full of wonder, and I love exploring it in all its forms! When I'm not delving into the wisdom of religious texts and sharing those teachings through preaching, you might find me curled up with a good book (historical fiction is a personal favorite!). But knowledge isn't the only thing that feeds my soul. I find immense joy in spending time outdoors, whether it's hiking through breathtaking landscapes or simply tending to my little herb garden. And let's not forget the power of a good cup of tea and a captivating documentary! There's something truly enriching about learning new things, no matter the source. Ultimately, my life is a beautiful blend of faith, exploration, and a sprinkle of cozy hobbies.
